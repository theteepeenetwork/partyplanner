PartyPlanner

The UK Event Services Marketplace

PartyPlanner is a UK marketplace where customers create an event, get instant pricing, and book multiple suppliers in one place. Unlike traditional directories built on enquiries, PartyPlanner runs on structured pricing and automated quotes — suppliers set their pricing rules once, and customers receive accurate quotes instantly, with minimal back-and-forth.

What We Do

We connect people planning events with the suppliers who deliver them, and remove the slowest part of the process: getting a price. Customers build a complete event package across several vendors and check out together. Suppliers win qualified, ready-to-book work instead of chasing unsuitable enquiries.

This is a marketplace, not an event-planning agency. Customers book services directly — not consultations.

Our Core Difference: Automated Quotes

Suppliers define pricing rules once. The platform then calculates an instant, itemised quote from:

  • Guest numbers & event duration
  • Packages, optional extras & quantities
  • Coverage area & travel charges

The result is faster bookings, transparent pricing, and far less manual admin on both sides.

Pricing Models

Every service uses exactly one structured, machine-readable model:

Guest-basedDuration-based Package tiersQuantity-based Public eventPrivate event

Pricing uses fixed numerical ranges (e.g. 1–50, 51–100) — never vague "100+" bands — so automation stays reliable. Public and private pricing are deliberately separate systems.

Who It's For

Event types: weddings, birthdays, christenings, corporate events, conferences, summer fairs, community events and private parties.

Supplier categories: catering & food vans, photography & videography, photobooths, DJs, musicians & entertainment, makeup, transport, stationery, gifts, chair covers, LED dance floors, illuminated letters, amusement rides and more. Categories are centrally managed for consistency.

Smart Tools for Suppliers

  • Quote automation — auto-accept bookings under a set value, within lead time, within travel radius and matching allowed event types.
  • Coverage areas — base location, radius and travel fees control where you appear and what you charge.
  • Analytics & earnings — track quotes generated, accepted/declined, upcoming bookings and payouts.
  • Optional extras & package tiers to upsell cleanly.

Payments & Booking

Customers secure a complete, multi-vendor booking with a 15% deposit at checkout, with the balance payable in full or by installments. Payments run through Stripe (optional — the platform works without it). Post-booking messaging keeps vendor–customer communication on-platform, with moderation built in.

Our Principles

  • Transparency — clear pricing, clear coverage, clear bookings.
  • Efficiency — suppliers spend less time quoting and filtering.
  • Fair exposure — visibility rotates fairly; the marketplace is never pay-to-win.
  • Distinct journeys — customer and supplier experiences stay separate by design.
